ST. ANTONY'S COLLEGE PERUVANTHANAM

St. Antony’s College is a self-financing institution located in the town of Peruvanthanam (Idukki district) Accredited by NAAC, which is famous for its Flora and Fauna. It was established in 2013. The College is affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. The College offers Undergraduate Courses in Arts, Science, Commerce and Management. Also, it offers postgraduate courses in commerce.

UG Programmes

  • Bachelor Of Computer Application (BCA)
  • Association Of Chartered Certified Accountants -(ACCA)
  • Bachelor Of Business Administration (BBA)
  • B.Com Model Ii Logistics Management
  • B.Sc Cyber Forensics
